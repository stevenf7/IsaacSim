.. _isaac_assets_environments_simple_grid:

Simple Grid
###########
This simple environment contains a flat ground and sides with a grid texture. Three configurations are provided; the first two have square corners, the third has curved corners.

.. list-table::

    * - .. figure:: /images/isaac_assets_default_grid_room.png
            :align: center
            :alt: Square Grid Room
            :width: 100%

            search ``default_environment.usd`` in the Content Browser or using the create menu: *Create>Environments>Flat Grid*

        Flat Grid.

      - .. figure:: /images/isaac_assets_square_grid_room.png
            :align: center
            :alt: Square Grid Room
            :width: 100%

            search ``gridroom_black.usd`` in the Content Browser or using the create menu: *Create>Environments>Black Grid*

        Black Grid.

      - .. figure:: /images/isaac_assets_curved_grid_room.png
            :align: center
            :alt: Curved Grid Room
            :width: 100%

            search ``gridroom_curved.usd`` in the Content Browser

        Curved Grid.

.. _isaac_assets_environments_warehouse:

Warehouse
###############
A warehouse environment with shelving and objects that can be placed on them. Four configurations are provided:

.. list-table::

    * - .. figure:: /images/isaac_assets_simple_warehouse.png
            :align: center
            :alt: Simple Warehouse
            :width: 100%

            search ``warehouse.usd`` in the Content Browser.

        A small warehouse with a single shelf.

      - .. figure:: /images/isaac_assets_simple_warehouse.png
            :align: center
            :alt: Simple Warehouse
            :width: 100%

            search ``warehouse_with_forklifts.usd`` in the Content Browser.

        A small warehouse with a single shelf and forklifts.

    * - .. figure:: /images/isaac_assets_simple_warehouse_multiple_shelves.png
            :align: center
            :alt: Small warehouse multiple shelves
            :width: 100%

            search ``warehouse_multiple_shelves.usd`` in the Content Browser.

        A small warehouse with multiple shelves.

      - .. figure:: /images/isaac_assets_full_warehouse.png
            :align: center
            :alt: Full Warehouse
            :width: 100%

            search ``full_warehouse.usd`` in the Content Browser.

        A full-sized warehouse with shelves, obstacles on the floors, and forklifts.
